About Me

Hello and welcome to my page! My name is Adam Glachan and I am a creative Front End Developer and Designer from Australia.

Over the last 10 years I have studied and worked in the Video Games Industry in a variety of roles including Design, 3D Art, Gameplay Prototyping and UI/UX Design, in addition to designing small websites and marketing material for local businesses in my area. As a result I have developed a broad range of skills and experience which I apply in all of my creative endeavours.

I am also a father of two and in my spare time I enjoy hanging out with my family, training and teaching traditional karate, playing video games and reading.
